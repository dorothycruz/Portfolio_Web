Blender

Blender is a dating app that gives people the option to meet at a restaurant. It’s a hybrid of both OpenTable and Tinder concepts.

You would be able to log in with your email or phone number, or with facebook if you don’t want to sign up. It would only use your email and name from facebook.

You would have two search bars on the top. On the first bar, you input the name of a resturant you know, a specific cuisine or a special feature. On the second, you can input the location.

It narrows the result by: Perfect Match - Someone that has the same favorites and the same time available. Favorites - anyone that have favorites the same restaurants that you have fav. Now - anyone who wants to eat at that time when you’re looking.

Then you will see potential matches and if you’re interested, you swipe right to 'like' them or hit the heart button. If not, go left to 'pass' or hit the X. If they've also 'liked' you, it's a match and you can start messaging.
